---

## Use Case

You follow a blog or newsletter and want to batch import recent articles into NotebookLM for knowledge organization.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get the RSS link">
    Find the blog's RSS feed URL. Common formats:
    - `/feed` — WordPress blogs
    - `/rss` — general
    - `/atom.xml` — Atom format
    - `medium.com/feed/@user` — Medium
    - Substack newsletters are usually at `/feed`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste into Jetpack">
    Click the Jetpack icon, switch to the "More" tab, go to RSS Import, paste the link, and click "Load".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select articles">
    Once loaded, you'll see the article list. Select all or check individual articles. Each entry shows the title and publication date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import">
    Click "Import Selected Articles" and the extension imports each article link into NotebookLM one by one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Formats

- RSS 2.0
- RSS 1.0
- Atom

<Tip>
  **Can't find an RSS link?** Try appending `/feed`, `/rss`, or `/atom.xml` to the domain. You can also use browser extensions like RSSHub Radar to auto-discover RSS feeds.
</Tip>
