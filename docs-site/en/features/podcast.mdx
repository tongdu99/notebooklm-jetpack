## The Problem

A two-hour podcast is exhausting to summarize manually. You want NotebookLM to generate notes for you, but it doesn't support pasting a podcast link directly.

## The Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get the podcast link">
    Copy a show or episode link from Apple Podcasts or Xiaoyuzhou FM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste into Jetpack">
    Click the Jetpack icon, switch to the "Podcast" tab, paste the link, and click "Search".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select episodes">
    The extension displays show info and an episode list. Check the episodes you want to download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download audio">
    Click "Download Selected" and the audio files will be saved locally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import into NotebookLM">
    Drag the downloaded audio files into your NotebookLM notebook. The AI will automatically generate complete notes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Link Format | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Apple Podcasts | `podcasts.apple.com/.../id123456` | Supports both show and episode links |
| Xiaoyuzhou FM | `xiaoyuzhoufm.com/episode/...` | Supports episode links |
| Xiaoyuzhou FM | `xiaoyuzhoufm.com/podcast/...` | Supports show links |

## Query Modes

- **Latest** — shows the most recently published episodes
- **All** — shows every episode (may be a long list)
- **Season** — filter by season or collection

<Tip>
  Downloaded audio files are typically in `.m4a` or `.mp3` format, both of which NotebookLM supports.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Audio files can be large (50-100 MB per episode). Make sure you have enough disk space and bandwidth.
</Warning>
