## The Problem

You discussed a technical architecture with ChatGPT, did a deep dive on a research paper with Claude, and brainstormed ideas with Gemini. These conversations are knowledge, but NotebookLM can't import AI chats directly.

## The Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open an AI chat page">
    Open a conversation on Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract the conversation">
    Click the Jetpack icon and switch to the "AI Chat" tab. The extension automatically detects the current platform.

    Click "Extract Current Conversation" and the extension pulls all Q&A pairs from the page DOM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Q&A pairs">
    Check the Q&A pairs you want to keep. Each pair shows a question summary and answer preview, so you can import only the valuable parts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import">
    Click "Import N Selected Q&A Pairs". The extension formats the conversation as structured Markdown and imports it into NotebookLM using the "Copied text" method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Domain | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Claude | `claude.ai` | Fully supported |
| ChatGPT | `chatgpt.com` | Fully supported |
| Gemini | `gemini.google.com` | Fully supported |

## Import Format

Content imported into NotebookLM is formatted as:

```markdown
# Conversation Title

**Source**: Claude conversation
**URL**: https://claude.ai/chat/xxx

---

## Human
Your question here...

## Claude
AI's response here...

---
```

## Share Cards

Beyond importing into NotebookLM, you can also turn great conversations into **share cards** -- perfect for posting on social media.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Q&A pairs">
    After extracting a conversation in the AI Chat tab, check the Q&A pairs you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate share card">
    Click the "Generate Share Card" button to preview the card on a new page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Click "Save JPEG" to export a high-resolution image. The dropdown menu also supports PNG, PDF, and copy to clipboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Card features:**
- Warm book-style typography (Noto Serif SC serif font, cream background, rust-red accent color)
- iPhone Dynamic Island safe area support (can be toggled off), great for mobile screenshot sharing
- 3x Retina high-resolution rendering
- Automatic Chinese/English layout adaptation

<Tip>
  **Selective import**: A long conversation often contains only a few gems. Use the checkbox feature to filter out small talk and keep only the best parts.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Make sure you have a NotebookLM notebook open before importing.
</Note>
